Latitude vs. Wind Speed


The purpose of this project was to analyze how the weather changes as you get closer to the equator. We see that our sample data has more points above the equator than below. This chart shows the location in respect to the equator has no direct or consistent affect on the speed of the wind on the sample dates within the dataset.
